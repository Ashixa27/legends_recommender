    What is League Of Legends?

    League of Legends is a free-to-play multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed by Riot Games. It features two teams of five players, each selecting unique champions with distinct abilities. Players compete on a map called Summoner’s Rift, aiming to destroy the opposing team's Nexus. The game requires strategic coordination, individual skill, and teamwork. With a global player base and a prominent esports scene, it remains one of the most popular competitive games worldwide.
